Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

Go to the login screen and tap "Forgot Password". You'll receive an email with instructions to reset it safely.

How do sessions work?

A "Session" represents a single poker game evening. You create a session, add players, and track their buy-ins. When the game ends, you enter their cash-out amounts, and the app calculates the results.

What personal data do you collect?

We only collect essential information such as your email address and phone number for account authentication. We also collect anonymous usage data to improve app stability.

Do you sell my personal data?

No. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. Your information is used solely to provide and improve the service.

Can I play for real money online?

No. Poker Track is strictly a tracking tool for offline, social games played privately among friends. It does not support online gameplay or real-money wagering.
